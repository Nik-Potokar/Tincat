FAQ

What platforms is the app available on?
The app is currently available on both iOS and Android platforms.
Is there a cost to use the app?
The app is available for free on the App Store and Google Play, with optional in-app purchases available.
How often are updates released for the app?
We release updates regularly to improve the user experience and add new features. Be sure to keep your app up to date for the best experience.
Can I use the app offline?
Some features of the app may be available offline, but an internet connection is required for full functionality.
Is the app secure?
Yes, the app is designed with security in mind and uses industry-standard encryption to protect your data. For more information on our security measures, please see our Privacy Policy.

If you encounter a bug or issue with the app, please contact our support team through the in-app support system or by emailing support@example.com. Our team will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
How do I get in touch with your customer support?

Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer refunds within 14 days of purchase. Please contact us for more information.
How long does it take to receive a response to my inquiry?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
